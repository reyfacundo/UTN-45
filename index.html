INTRODUCTION

'I wanted to tell you that wherever you may end up in this world, I will be searching for you.'

Directed by Makoto Shinkai, 'Kimi no Na wa,' or 'Your Name,' is a Japanese animated film that beautifully blends romance, fantasy, and drama. It follows the lives of two teenagers, Taki and Mitsuha, living in different worlds but sharing a profound connection.

Taki, a high school boy in Tokyo, and Mitsuha, a high school girl in a rural town, experience a mysterious phenomenon: they swap bodies sporadically, leaving them baffled. While navigating each other's lives, they learn about challenges, dreams, and responsibilities, forming a deep bond through shared notes.

As their connection deepens, the body-swapping stops, leaving them longing for each other. Taki embarks on a quest to reunite with Mitsuha.

MITSUHA

Mitsuha is a lively high school girl from a charming rural town. She's got big dreams and a sense of adventure that reaches far beyond her hometown, where everyone knows everyone else.

TAKI

Taki is a diligent high school student living in Tokyo. He's ambitious, hardworking, and always on the go, striving to achieve his dreams in the fast-paced city.
